Title: Piracetam for Treatment of Cocaine Addiction - 3

Official Title: Piracetam for Treatment of Cocaine Addiction

Summary: 
      The purpose of this study is to evaluate the effects of IV cocaine administration in cocaine
      dependent subjects maintained on piracetam while they are residing in an inpatient
      addictions treatment research unit and to subsequently follow these patients in an eight
      week open label assessment of piracetam in an outpatient treatment program.
